Tocino Breakfast Sandwich

15 mins prep 2 servings Easy

Start your morning with this delicious Filipino-inspired breakfast sandwich featuring our sweet JCL Tocino paired with fluffy eggs and melted cheese on toasted bread. This satisfying sandwich brings together the perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors to energize your day.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices JCL Tocino
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 English muffins or sandwich rolls
  • 2 slices Cheese (cheddar or American)
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Banana ketchup (optional)
  • 4 leaves Lettuce
  • 2 slices Tomato
  • To taste Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the tocino: In a pan over medium heat, add 1/4 cup water and the tocino slices. Simmer uncovered for 5-6 minutes until water evaporates and tocino caramelizes, turning occasionally. Remove from pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, melt 1/2 tbsp butter and fry eggs to your preferred doneness (sunny-side-up or over-easy works well). Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toast the English muffins or sandwich rolls until golden brown.
  4. Spread mayonnaise (and banana ketchup if using) on the bottom halves of the toasted muffins.
  5. Layer each sandwich with lettuce, tomato slice, cooked tocino, fried egg, and cheese slice.
  6. Place the top halves of the muffins on the sandwiches and press gently.
  7. If desired, return the assembled sandwiches to the pan over low heat for 1 minute to melt the cheese.
  8. Serve immediately while warm.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add sautéed onions or mushrooms for extra flavor. For a spicy kick, include sliced jalapeños or a dash of hot sauce.

Storage: Best enjoyed fresh. The cooked tocino can be refrigerated for up to 3 days.

Serving Suggestion: Pair with fresh fruit or hash browns for a complete breakfast meal.
