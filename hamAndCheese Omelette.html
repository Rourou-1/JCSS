Ham and Cheese Omelette

15 mins prep 2 servings Easy

A hearty breakfast featuring our premium JCL Slice Ham with fluffy eggs and melted cheese - the perfect way to start your day or enjoy a leisurely weekend brunch. This classic combination delivers protein-packed satisfaction in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large eggs
  • 100g JCL Slice Ham, diced
  • 1/2 cup Shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Milk or cream
  • 1/4 cup Diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Diced onions (optional)
  • To taste Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp Fresh chives or parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, and a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. If using, sauté onions and bell peppers for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  4. Add diced JCL ham and cook for another 1-2 minutes to warm through.
  5. Remove ham and vegetables from skillet and set aside.
  6. Add remaining butter to the skillet and pour in the egg mixture.
  7. As eggs begin to set, gently push cooked portions toward the center with a spatula, tilting pan to allow uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  8. When eggs are nearly set (about 2-3 minutes), sprinkle the ham mixture and cheese over one half of the omelette.
  9. Carefully fold the other half over the filling with the spatula.
  10. Cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute until cheese begins to melt.
  11. Slide onto a plate, garnish with fresh herbs, and serve immediately.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add sautéed mushrooms or spinach for extra vegetables. For a richer omelette, use half-and-half instead of milk.

Storage: Best enjoyed fresh. If needed, refrigerate for up to 1 day and reheat gently in a skillet.

Serving Suggestion: Serve with toasted bread, fresh fruit, or a simple green salad for a complete meal.
