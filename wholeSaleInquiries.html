Wholesale Inquiries

Partner With JCL Production

Thank you for your interest in becoming a wholesale partner with JCL Production. We offer premium quality frozen foods to retailers, restaurants, caterers, and institutional food service providers at competitive wholesale prices.

Why Choose JCL Production?

  • Premium quality Filipino-inspired frozen foods
  • Consistent product standards
  • Flexible minimum order quantities
  • Reliable delivery schedules
  • Dedicated account management

Available Product Categories

  • Frozen Dim Sum: Pork Siomai, Chicken Siomai, and more
  • Frozen Meats: Slice Ham and specialty cuts
  • Finger Foods: Shanghai Lumpia and other appetizers
  • Specialty Items: Custom products available for volume orders

Note: Full product catalog with wholesale pricing available upon request.

Minimum Order Requirements

  • Initial order: ₱5,000 minimum purchase
  • Reorders: ₱3,000 minimum purchase
  • Bulk discounts: Available for orders exceeding ₱20,000

How to Apply

  1. Complete the wholesale application form below
  2. Submit required business documents (DTI/SEC registration, business permit, etc.)
  3. Schedule a product sampling session (in-person or by delivery)
  4. Receive your wholesale pricing and account details

Important: Please allow 3-5 business days for application processing and verification.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Metro Manila: Free delivery for orders over ₱10,000
  • Provincial: Shipping fees apply based on location
  • Lead time: 3-5 business days from order confirmation
  • Scheduled deliveries: Available for regular partners

Payment Terms

  • New accounts: 50% deposit, 50% upon delivery
  • Established accounts: Net 15 or Net 30 terms available after 3 successful orders
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer, check, or online payment
