Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Your Questions

We've compiled answers to the most common questions our customers ask. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact us.

Product Information

Are your products preservative-free?

Our products contain minimal preservatives to ensure food safety and reasonable shelf life. We use natural preservatives whenever possible and avoid artificial additives. Each product has its ingredient list clearly marked on the packaging.

How long do your products last in the refrigerator?

Our products have varying shelf lives:

  • Siomai (pork and chicken): 2-3 days refrigerated, up to 3 months frozen
  • Shanghai Lumpia: 3-4 days refrigerated, up to 6 months frozen
  • Sliced Ham: 5-7 days refrigerated when properly sealed
  • Tocino and Longaniza: 7 days refrigerated, up to 3 months frozen

Always check the expiration date on the packaging for specific guidance.

Do you have halal or vegetarian options?

We currently offer limited vegetarian options. Our main product line consists of meat-based products. We are working on developing more vegetarian alternatives in the future. None of our current products are halal certified.

How should I cook your products?

Cooking instructions are provided on each package. For detailed cooking methods and recipe suggestions, please visit our Recipes page where we provide step-by-step instructions and cooking videos.

Orders & Shipping

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to:

  • Tarlac City and surrounding areas (same-day delivery available)
  • Metro Manila (1-2 days delivery time)
  • Other Luzon areas (2-3 days delivery time)

For Visayas and Mindanao, we use third-party shipping services with delivery times of 3-5 days. For complete shipping information, please check our Shipping Policy.

How do you ensure products remain fresh during shipping?

We use insulated packaging with ice packs to maintain proper temperature during transit. All perishable products are shipped frozen or chilled depending on the product type. Our packaging is designed to keep products at safe temperatures for up to 48 hours under normal conditions.

What is the minimum order amount?

Our minimum order requirements are:

  • Regular orders: No minimum amount
  • Free shipping: Orders over ₱1,000 within Tarlac
  • Free shipping: Orders over ₱2,500 for Metro Manila
  • Free shipping: Orders over ₱3,500 for other areas

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number on our website's "Order Tracking" page or directly with the courier's tracking system. For local Tarlac deliveries done by our team, we'll provide real-time updates via text message.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Digital Wallets (GCash, PayMaya, GrabPay)
  • Bank Transfers (BDO, BPI, UnionBank, MetroBank)
  • Cash on Delivery (selected areas in Tarlac)
  • Payment Centers (7-Eleven, SM Payment Center, Palawan Express)

For detailed information, please see our Payment Options page.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Yes, our website uses SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) requirements to ensure secure transactions. We never store your complete credit card information on our servers.

Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery for orders within Tarlac City and immediate surrounding areas. For security and operational reasons, COD is not available for orders outside of our local delivery zone or for orders exceeding ₱5,000.

Do you charge any additional fees?

The only additional fee we charge is for shipping, which varies based on your location and order size. There are no hidden processing fees or surcharges. For certain payment methods like COD, a small convenience fee of ₱50 may apply.

Wholesale Information

How do I become a wholesale customer?

To become a wholesale customer:

  1. Fill out the wholesale application form on our Wholesale page
  2. Provide your business registration documents (DTI/SEC registration, Mayor's Permit, etc.)
  3. Our wholesale team will review your application (usually within 1-2 business days)
  4. Once approved, you'll receive wholesale login credentials and pricing information

What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale?

Our minimum order quantities for wholesale accounts are:

  • Initial order: ₱10,000 minimum
  • Subsequent orders: ₱5,000 minimum
  • For specific products: Minimum of 10 units/packs per variant

Higher volume orders qualify for additional discounts. Contact our wholesale department for more information.

Do you offer custom packaging for wholesale orders?

Yes, we offer custom packaging and private labeling for wholesale customers with orders exceeding ₱100,000 per month. Custom packaging requires a lead time of 4-6 weeks for initial orders and has minimum quantity requirements. Contact our wholesale department for details and pricing.

What are your payment terms for wholesale accounts?

Our standard payment terms for wholesale accounts are:

  • New accounts: 50% deposit, 50% upon delivery
  • Established accounts (after 6 months): Net 15 or Net 30 terms available after credit evaluation
  • Volume discounts: Applied automatically based on order size

For detailed information, please contact our wholesale department at wholesale@jclproduction.com.
