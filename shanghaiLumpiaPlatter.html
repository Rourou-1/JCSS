Shanghai Lumpia Platter

25 mins prep 4-5 servings Medium

Serve our crispy JCL Shanghai Lumpia with a sweet chili sauce and fresh vegetables for a perfect party appetizer or main dish. This platter presentation makes it ideal for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 20 pieces JCL Shanghai Lumpia
  • 1 cup Sweet chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup Vinegar
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Crushed red pepper (optional)
  • 2 cups Vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 head Lettuce, shredded
  • 1 Cucumber, julienned
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 1/4 cup Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dipping sauce: In a small bowl, mix sweet chili sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, soy sauce, and crushed red pepper (if using). Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or wok to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Fry the lumpia in batches (about 5-6 pieces at a time) for 3-4 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Remove lumpia from oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  5. Prepare the platter: Arrange shredded lettuce as a base on a large serving plate.
  6. Place the fried lumpia on top of the lettuce bed.
  7. Arrange julienned cucumber and carrot around the lumpia for color and freshness.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley.
  9. Serve immediately with the prepared dipping sauce on the side.

Chef's Notes

Variations: For extra crunch, serve with crushed peanuts sprinkled on top. For a complete meal, serve with steamed rice.

Storage: Uncooked lumpia can be frozen for up to 3 months. Fried lumpia are best served immediately but can be refrigerated for 1 day and reheated in the oven.

Serving Suggestion: Perfect for parties as finger food or as part of a larger Filipino meal spread.
