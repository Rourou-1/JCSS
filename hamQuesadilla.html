Ham and Cheese Quesadilla

15 mins prep 2 servings Easy

Transform our premium JCL Slice Ham into a deliciously crispy quesadilla with melted cheese - the perfect quick meal or snack that comes together in just minutes. This simple yet satisfying recipe is versatile enough for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 Flour tortillas (8-inch)
  • 150g JCL Slice Ham, diced or sliced thin
  • 1 1/2 cups Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or blend)
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 cup Diced bell peppers (optional)
  • 2 tbsp Diced onions (optional)
  • 1 tsp Taco seasoning or paprika (optional)
  • To taste Salt and pepper
  • For serving Sour cream, salsa, guacamole

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. If using vegetables, sauté bell peppers and onions in a little oil until softened, about 3 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Lightly butter one side of each tortilla.
  4. Place one tortilla butter-side down in the skillet. Sprinkle with 1/4 of the cheese.
  5. Add half of the JCL ham evenly over the cheese, followed by half of the sautéed vegetables (if using). Sprinkle with taco seasoning if desired.
  6. Top with another 1/4 of the cheese and place a second tortilla on top, butter-side up.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bottom is golden brown, then carefully flip using a spatula.
  8. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the second side is golden and cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from skillet and repeat with remaining ingredients for the second quesadilla.
  10. Let rest for 1 minute before cutting into wedges.
  11. Serve warm with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole on the side.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add sliced jalapeños for heat or diced tomatoes for freshness. For breakfast version, add scrambled eggs.

Storage: Best enjoyed immediately. Leftovers can be refrigerated for 1 day and reheated in a skillet.

Serving Suggestion: Pair with a simple side salad or Mexican rice for a complete meal.
