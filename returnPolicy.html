Return & Refund Policy

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

At JCL Production, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we'll make it right.

Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns under these conditions:

  • Product received damaged or spoiled
  • Incorrect item received
  • Quality issues (discoloration, odd smell, etc.)

Important: We cannot accept returns due to customer preference changes or improper storage after delivery.

Timeframe for Returns

  • Perishable items: Must report within 12 hours of delivery
  • Non-perishable items: Within 3 days of delivery

Return Process

  1. Contact us immediately at (0951) 642-8442 or returns@jclproduction.com
  2. Provide photos of the issue and your receipt
  3. We'll arrange for pickup (for damaged goods) or provide return instructions
  4. Once received, we'll process your refund or replacement

Refund Methods

  • Original payment: Refunded to your original payment method (3-5 business days)
  • Store credit: Instant JCL Production e-voucher
  • Replacement: Priority shipping for replacement items

Note: Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Damaged During Shipping

If your package arrives damaged:

  • Refuse delivery if visibly damaged
  • Take photos before opening
  • Contact us immediately - we'll send a replacement at no cost

Quality Assurance

To ensure the best quality, all returns are carefully inspected by our quality control team. We continuously improve our packaging and shipping processes based on return patterns to minimize future issues.
