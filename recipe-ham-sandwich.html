Ultimate Ham Sandwich

10 mins prep 2 servings Easy

Elevate your sandwich game with this simple yet delicious combination that highlights our premium JCL Slice Ham. Perfect for quick lunches, picnics, or hearty snacks, this recipe balances flavors and textures for maximum enjoyment.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices Fresh bread (your choice)
  • 200g JCL Slice Ham
  • 4 slices Cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 4 leaves Crisp lettuce
  • 4 slices Tomato
  • 4 slices Cucumber
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • To taste Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise and Dijon mustard to create your sandwich spread.
  2. Lightly toast the bread slices until golden brown for extra texture and flavor.
  3. Spread the mayo-mustard mixture on one side of each bread slice.
  4. Layer the JCL Slice Ham evenly on two of the bread slices.
  5. Add cheese slices on top of the ham, followed by lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
  6. Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  7. Top with the remaining bread slices, spread side down.
  8. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt the butter.
  9. Grill each sandwich for 2-3 minutes per side until the bread is golden and the cheese begins to melt.
  10. Remove from heat, cut diagonally, and serve immediately.
