Siomai Rice Bowl

20 mins prep 3-4 servings Medium

Create a complete and satisfying meal by pairing our premium JCL siomai with fragrant garlic rice and a special dipping sauce. This recipe brings together the best of Filipino-Chinese flavors in one comforting bowl.

Ingredients

  • 20 pieces JCL Pork or Chicken Siomai
  • 3 cups Cooked rice
  • 6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp Chili garlic sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 cup Green onions, chopped
  • 1 Egg, fried (per serving)
  • To taste Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Steam the JCL siomai according to package instructions (about 10-12 minutes).
  2. While siomai is steaming, prepare garlic rice: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and sauté minced garlic until golden brown and fragrant.
  3. Add cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the garlic. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Keep warm.
  4. Prepare the dipping sauce: In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, sesame oil, and chili garlic sauce (if using). Mix well.
  5. Assemble the bowls: Place a generous portion of garlic rice in each bowl, top with 5-6 pieces of steamed siomai.
  6. Add a fried egg on top of each bowl (optional but recommended).
  7. Sprinkle with chopped green onions.
  8. Serve immediately with the dipping sauce on the side.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add sautéed vegetables like carrots and green beans for extra nutrition. For a richer version, drizzle with a little oyster sauce.

Storage: Components can be stored separately in the refrigerator for up to 2 days. Reheat rice and siomai separately before serving.

Serving Suggestion: Pair with atchara (pickled papaya) or sliced fresh cucumbers for contrast.
