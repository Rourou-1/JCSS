Shipping Policy

Delivery Information

At JCL Production, we strive to deliver your premium processed meats as quickly and safely as possible. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following locations:

  • Concepcion, Tarlac: Free delivery for orders over ₱500
  • Nearby Tarlac towns: Capas, Bamban, Tarlac City (delivery fee applies)
  • Other areas: Available via courier (LBC, J&T Express)

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays). Orders placed after 2PM will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Methods & Costs

  • Standard Delivery (Concepcion): ₱50 (Free for orders ₱500+)
  • Nearby Towns: ₱100-₱150 depending on location
  • Courier Nationwide: Calculated at checkout (based on weight)

Delivery Timeframes

  • Concepcion: Same day for orders before 10AM
  • Nearby Areas: 1-2 business days
  • Nationwide: 2-5 business days via courier

Note: During peak seasons (Christmas, New Year, Fiestas), delivery times may extend by 1-2 days.

Order Tracking

For courier deliveries, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS/email once your order ships. For local deliveries, our driver will contact you 30 minutes before arrival.

Perishable Goods Handling

All meat products are packed with ice gel packs in insulated containers to maintain freshness during transit. We recommend refrigerating immediately upon receipt.
