Siomai Stir Fry with Vegetables

20 mins prep 3-4 servings Easy

A quick and healthy stir fry that combines our flavorful JCL siomai with crisp mixed vegetables in a savory sauce. This dish comes together in minutes but delivers restaurant-quality taste perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 20 pieces JCL Pork or Chicken Siomai
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 Bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup Broccoli florets
  • 1 cup Snow peas or green beans
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 2 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup Water or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch (optional, for thicker sauce)
  • To taste Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Steam the siomai according to package instructions (about 10 minutes). Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add garlic and onion, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots and stir-fry for 1 minute, then add bell peppers, broccoli, and snow peas. Cook for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Push vegetables to one side of the wok. Add the steamed siomai and lightly pan-fry for 1-2 minutes to get a slight crispness.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together oyster sauce, soy sauce, sesame oil, and water. Pour over the stir fry.
  6. If using cornstarch, mix it with 1 tbsp water and add to the wok to thicken the sauce.
  7. Toss everything together gently and cook for another 1-2 minutes until sauce coats everything evenly.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Transfer to a serving platter, garnish with sesame seeds, and serve hot with steamed rice.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add mushrooms or baby corn for extra vegetables. For spice, add chili flakes or sriracha to the sauce.

Storage: Refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to 2 days. Reheat gently in a skillet.

Serving Suggestion: Serve over steamed jasmine rice or noodles for a complete meal.
