Creamy Ham Pasta

25 mins prep 3-4 servings Easy

Transform our premium JCL Slice Ham into a restaurant-quality pasta dish with this simple yet impressive creamy pasta recipe. The perfect balance of savory ham, rich cream sauce, and al dente pasta makes this a family favorite that comes together in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 400g Pasta (penne, fettuccine, or your choice)
  • 200g JCL Slice Ham, diced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 cup Frozen peas (optional)
  • To taste Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, sautéing until soft and fragrant (about 3 minutes).
  3. Add diced JCL ham and cook for another 2 minutes until slightly browned.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and milk, stirring to combine. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add Dijon mustard and nutmeg (if using), stirring well to incorporate.
  6. Gradually add Parmesan cheese, stirring constantly until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  7. If using peas, add them now and cook for 1-2 minutes until heated through.
  8. Add the drained pasta to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly. If sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until desired consistency is reached.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan cheese before serving.

Chef's Notes

Variations: Add sautéed mushrooms or spinach for extra vegetables. For a lighter version, substitute half the cream with chicken broth.

Storage: Refrigerate leftovers in an airtight container for up to 2 days. Reheat gently with a splash of milk to refresh the sauce.

Serving Suggestion: Serve with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.
